The Establishment of The Company

Mr. Luthful Ikram Litu founded PALME GROUP SRLS between 2020 and 2025 in Palermo, Italy, transforming his vision of a multinational enterprise into reality by creating a company that bridges Italy and Bangladesh, fosters innovation, and promotes global business collaboration.

Luthful Ikram Litu is a visionary entrepreneur and founder of PALME GROUP SRLS, dedicated to building innovative multinational ventures that connect Italy and Bangladesh, fostering growth, technology, and global collaboration.
